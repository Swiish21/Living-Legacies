Our Mission

To preserve, celebrate, and share the rich cultural heritage of Kenya's diverse communities through an accessible digital platform that educates, inspires, and connects people with the living cultures that make Kenya unique.

üåü

Our Vision

A world where Kenya's cultural diversity is celebrated, understood, and preserved for future generations, fostering national unity while honoring the unique traditions of each community.

Our Story

Born from a passion to preserve Kenya's rich cultural tapestry, Living Legacies emerged as a digital sanctuary where tradition meets technology.

2020

The Beginning

Our founder, inspired by conversations with elders from various communities, recognized the urgent need to document and preserve Kenya's cultural heritage before it's lost to time.

2021

Community Connection

We began collaborating with community leaders, cultural experts, and artisans to gather authentic stories, traditions, and knowledge from each ethnic group.

2022

Digital Platform Launch

Living Legacies came to life as a comprehensive digital platform, featuring detailed community profiles, cultural practices, and integrated artisan marketplaces.

2024

Growing Impact

Today, we've reached millions of people globally, creating bridges between cultures and supporting artisan communities across Kenya through our marketplace integration.
